## Venn Exchange / BEE — the cross-vertical commerce and capital layer of VENN Bioventures

**Identity:** Venn Exchange is not a marketplace — it is a firewall with a single auditable door: the one venue where a conservation fact that was earned under a money-blind constitution is allowed to meet capital and industry through an immutable, citable provenance receipt, one-directional, never flowing back into the science.

**North star:** One verified value output, provably never money-edited, crosses the firewall and reaches a real counterparty — and the immutable provenance receipt of that crossing becomes the unit everyone trusts. Move the count of real, ABS-clear, citable opportunities from 0 to 1, honestly, before scaling anything.

**Unique edge:** The firewall-as-wedge: copying the Exchange requires first building a money-blind conservation engine upstream — years of work and a constitution a competitor does not have. The inimitable asset is the frozen, money-blind, citable provenance receipt (a GBIF/protocols.io-style DOI-bearing evidence object) that guarantees to a diligence team: this value claim's underlying evidence was provably never edited by the prospect of money, and (for medicinal material) carries benefit-sharing/ABS consent. No generic deal board can offer that; an AngelList-for-impact can be cloned in a weekend, the receipt cannot. Lead with the receipt, not the floor.
