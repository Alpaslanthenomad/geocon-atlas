# GEOCON v-next — the portfolio-first reconception

> 8-agent brainstorm: current-flow audit + a 5-lens bold panel + adjudicated synthesis. Strategy + experience first.

## The new version
THE BOOK — a researcher's personal Conservation Book. Reconception in three sentences: GEOCON v-next stops being a platform you operate and becomes a Book you hold open on your own page — a portfolio where the atomic unit is a POSITION (one species × one work-area, the work-area being literally the X=safeguard / Y=knowledge / Z=value axis you already shipped). You build the Book first by taking positions (free, reversible, zero-ceremony — a watch-row is already a faint position); evidence flows into a position only through the shipped VERB (every minted receipt visibly raises that position's evidence band from 0); and when a position earns conviction you PROMOTE it — which is just `create_program`, pre-filled — turning it into a managed program that runs on the unchanged Venn engine. A program is never a new place you enter and follow a fixed route; it is a position you decided to actively manage, and it is structurally forbidden to drift: it must reach an EXIT that is either a Conservation Win (X/Y positions) or a Commercial Output (Z positions), and only a verified Commercial Output crosses the firewall into the Exchange as a money-blind Success Story. One noun (position), four altitudes (watch → active → managed → exited), one vocabulary.

JUDGMENT OF THE PANEL (scored honestly): #1 The Book / fund-manager (9/10) — the only proposal that gets the spine exactly right: portfolio = positions, program = a promoted position (not a sibling object), evidence-equity = the shipped 0–1 bands, two honest exits, axis pre-commits the exit type. Its single risk is metaphor-leakage of money language onto conservation. #5 The Stake / ledger-of-standing (8/10) — the strongest single idea in the whole panel: make the OPEN QUESTION the ownable unit, which realizes the North-Star ("the gap is the product") better than anything else; its weakness is it under-specifies promotion-to-program and risks re-litigating the reverted bench. #3 The Accession / living-collection (8/10) — the best felt-experience and the most honest reframe ("a program is a plant you already keep, grown serious"; cold-start hook "47,000 plants, none kept"; the conservation-or-commercial fork as the most honest *scientific* moment, gated by the evidence you actually have). #4 The Plot / studio (7/10) — cleanest IA collapse (five surfaces → one) and the best "growth-state" naming, but "Plot" is a weaker noun than position/question. #2 The Expedition / cartography (6/10) — most emotionally vivid (drawing a coastline out of dark water = the gap becoming visible) but the nautical skin is the highest tone-risk for a no-hype founder and the map is the heaviest build. SPINE = #1. GRAFTS: the OPEN-QUESTION framing from #5 (a position is phrased as a question with your name on it — this is what makes the Book emotionally yours and screenshot-worthy at cold start), the cold-start hook + "grown-serious" felt-experience from #3, the five-surfaces-into-one IA collapse from #4, and the evidence-fills-the-bar satisfaction loop common to #3/#5.

## The one surprising idea
The surprise is subtractive and it inverts the platform's center of gravity: the PROGRAM ENGINE — the thing the founder spent the most effort building — gets demoted to a sub-state of a single position, and the most neglected surface (the empty profile) becomes the most important one in the product: it becomes the Book, the researcher's trading desk and identity. Everyone, including the findings report and four of the five panels' default instinct, assumed the fix was to ADD a /portfolio page next to programs. The opposite is true. You don't add a portfolio; you make the portfolio the only noun and demote "program" to one thing a portfolio item BECOMES when you decide to manage it. And the deepest surprise is the reframe of identity for a fundless, zero-user, lone researcher: he is handed the felt experience of running a book with a thesis, conviction, compounding evidence, and an exit — a portfolio that is visibly going somewhere — while the money-blind firewall is not loosened to enable it but made LOAD-BEARING to the metaphor: the Book's currency is evidence, never cash, and that is precisely why an institution or VC can trust a Book on sight. The firewall stops being a constraint and becomes the product's credibility engine.

## Organizing system
ONE NOUN, FOUR ALTITUDES, ZERO new mental models the founder has to hold beyond this single rule: "Everything is a species × a work-area you've taken on, and the only thing that ever changes is how much evidence stands behind it and who is watching."

THE POSITION is the atom. A position = (researcher, species, axis ∈ {safeguard, knowledge, value}, conviction dial, status). It is phrased on the card as an open question with the researcher's name on it ("Sevket — is wild collection fragmenting Colchicum in Anatolia?" / Safeguard) — borrowing #5's open-question framing so the Book reads as a portrait of a mind, not a spreadsheet. Crucially, two numbers are kept rigorously separate to protect data-integrity: CONVICTION is the researcher's subjective dial (intent only, never credited, never sorts the Book); EVIDENCE-EQUITY is the objective, receipt-backed strength rendered in the exact shipped 0.00–1.00 bands (weak / moderate / strong / verified). The Book sorts and credits on evidence, never on conviction — so a thin book can never feel falsely momentous.

FOUR STATUSES are the only lifecycle: WATCH (free, reversible — this IS the existing watchlist row, re-skinned: a species you ★-saved is a faint Watch position) → ACTIVE (≥1 receipt has landed; the VERB auto-promotes a Watch into Active and the band lifts off 0) → MANAGED (you promoted it to a program; the Venn engine now runs underneath) → EXITED (the program reached its forced output: a Conservation Win or a Commercial Output).

FIVE SURFACES COLLAPSE INTO TWO (grafting #4's IA discipline): the Book (your page — replaces profile + workspace + the blank program-create form) and the Position detail (the only sub-surface — a Watch/Active position shows its receipts and one next move; a Managed position shows the program engine inside it). No more five disjoint panels.

THE TAPE is the spine's heartbeat (from #1): one reverse-chronological ledger across the whole Book — every receipt minted, band lifted, gate passed, endorsement received, output exited. It replaces the felt-aimlessness with visible motion at any scale, even one user, even one receipt.

The work-areas the founder asked for ARE the X/Y/Z axes, surfaced at last as a human choice you make when you take a position — not a hidden Pathways tab discovered after creation. The dormant 279-node chain becomes legible exactly where it belongs: it is the menu of "what a Value (Z) position can become" (taxonomy → propagation → extraction → metabolite), revealed only when you open a Z-position — solving the "chain is decoupled" gap without reviving the rejected 6-stage vocabulary.

## The spine (portfolio -> program -> output -> Exchange -> VC)
portfolio → work-areas → program → conservation/commercial output → Exchange success story → paid/market → VC, as ONE continuous object seen at rising altitude:

1) PORTFOLIO = THE BOOK. The researcher's own page is a grid of position cards. Taking a position is zero-ceremony (search a species, pick an axis, optionally phrase the question) and costs nothing — it is a Watch position, explicitly labelled OPEN until a receipt lands. Existing ★-watched species pre-seed the Book so it is never empty.

2) WORK-AREAS = the axis on each position (X safeguard / Y knowledge / Z value). "I'll work Colchicum on Knowledge and Allium on Value" is just opening two positions on two axes. The axis is the single most consequential choice because it pre-commits the eventual exit type.

3) PROGRAM = a position promoted to MANAGED. "Manage this position" calls the existing `create_program`, pre-filled from the position: species_id set, axis → default region, the question → strategic rationale, and the receipts already minted on that position attached as the program's opening evidence. The blank-form cold-start dies. The program is NOT a new beginning; it is the same position, now actively run, optionally with co-managers via the existing `request_to_join_program`. Owning vs joining = running your own book vs backing someone else's.

4) CONSERVATION/COMMERCIAL OUTPUT = the forced EXIT. A managed program runs the unchanged Venn engine (foundation→…→governance, tics, stage gates, xyz-as-maturity-not-gate — all verbatim). It cannot drift forever: reaching the deployment stage opens a terminal EXIT GATE (built from the existing gate machinery) that the program cannot pass un-forked. The axis decides which door opens first: X/Y-dominant → Conservation Win (a banked accession, a filed Red-List assessment, a published method — zero money columns, ever); Z-dominant → Commercial Output (a product, a propagation protocol, a license). The exit is declared via the existing `declare_commercialized_outcome` and climbs the existing ladder self_declared → peer_endorsed → org_declared → venn_verified. The rare xyz position that earned BOTH is the Book's celebrated ten-bagger.

5) EXCHANGE SUCCESS STORY = a verified output crossing the firewall, ONE-DIRECTIONAL and money-blind. A Conservation Win publishes to the public Outcomes feed + the species page (its reward is reputational and evidential; it does NOT enter the Exchange). A Commercial Output at venn_verified lists on the existing Exchange Deal Board as a Success Story — a narrative the platform auto-assembles from the position's own ledger (its receipts, its passed gates, its bands), the researcher's name as originator, price-free. The existing money/PII-blind CHECK on chain_evidence and the allowlist receipt projection are untouched; the Story only CITES conservation, read-only.

6) PAID/MARKET → VC = the visible tail. The admin-only door is replaced by a manager-visible CARRY lane on the Book: the originator watches "your Galanthus × Value output is listed → 2 funds viewing → deal room opened" via the existing `open_bridge_opportunity` → `TokenDealRoom`. Paid Exchange members are framed as co-investing in the verified pipeline; if an output needs a new company it escalates to a VC deal room (the Directory's real funds) or an investment call to large firms — both seeded by the Success Story, money never touching the conservation substrate.

## Felt experience
FOR THE LONE FOUNDER, DAY ONE: he opens his page and it is not an empty dashboard of five panels — it is his Book, mostly empty, with one quiet line at the top: "Take your first position. What plant, and what kind of work?" He searches Galanthus, picks the green Knowledge axis, and (optionally) types the open question he actually cares about. A position card appears — faint, banded at 0, stamped OPEN, with his name on it. It is his, and it is public as a question: the world can now see he owns that gap. The card whispers exactly one next move: "Back it — assert one fact." He runs the VERB he already knows (galantamine, DOI-backed), and the same card he just created visibly lifts: a receipt badge lands, the evidence band ticks weak→moderate, the position flips Watch→Active, and the Tape at the top of the Book records the first line — "Receipt GX-… minted · Galanthus × Knowledge → moderate." In five minutes a solo researcher has a desk that is his, one real evidenced fact, a citable receipt, and — critically — he can SEE the path ahead, because the card now whispers the next move: "Manage this — run it as a program."

THE DAILY RETURN: he comes back not to parse a toolkit but to read his Book — some positions faint (Watch, waiting), some Active with receipts, one or two Managed showing a calm progress ring and "what you're proving now" in plain language, maybe one glowing with an exit prompt. There is never aimlessness because every card states its own single next move and its own destination. The satisfaction is not points or confetti (the tone stays sober, field-notebook, no emojis) — it is the legible, compounding sense of a portfolio going somewhere: evidence accruing, bands climbing off 0, the Tape proving the Book is moving even at the scale of one person.

THE PAYOFF, AND THE GROWTH INTO TEAMS: the day he gets his first endorsement, a position visibly goes self-declared→peer-endorsed — his Book just earned external validation. When his first managed position reaches the exit gate, the platform asks the manager's question — "What did Colchicum × Value return — a Conservation Win or a Commercial Output?" — and writes his first Success Story for him from his own ledger. He watches it list on the Exchange and a fund start watching it; the Carry lane shows the deal room open with his name as originator. Teams arrive naturally: a position can show "two other researchers are working Allium in Anatolia," and inviting a co-manager is "sign onto this position." The Exchange and VC are growth-stage rewards layered on the SAME object, never a separate world he has to go learn. For the first time, a fundless conservation researcher gets the subjective experience of an investor with a winning book — conviction, momentum, an exit — without one dollar ever touching the conservation data.

## Reconciliation (one vocabulary, zero confusion)
Nothing is replaced; everything is re-narrated and connected through one spine, and the founder learns exactly ONE rule: a program IS a managed position. Direct, term-for-term mapping to shipped parts so there is zero confusion and zero second system:

VERB / RECEIPT (`assert_fact_and_mint` → `/receipt/[pid]`, money-blind CHECK on chain_evidence) → UNCHANGED. New behavior only: an asserted fact optionally carries a position_id, so the receipt attaches to a Book position (today it floats standalone — this finally gives it a home) and posts a line to the Tape and lifts that position's band. If the species isn't yet a position, asserting auto-creates an Active one. The firewall CHECK is untouched.

PROGRAM ENGINE (regions x/y/z…xyz; stages foundation→…→governance; tics; stage-based gates; evidence-strength 0–1; `get_program_region_status`, `get_program_stage_status`, `apply_move`, `complete_program_tic`; the law "xyz is a maturity target, never a gate") → UNCHANGED in DB and logic, verbatim to VENN-ENGINE-CONTRACT. No DB enum is renamed (this is the explicit guard against a third competing lexicon after the rejected 6-stage spine — the engineering terms simply retire from the UI rather than stacking).

OUTCOME LADDER (`declare_commercialized_outcome`, endorse RPCs, self→peer→org→venn) → UNCHANGED. New only: it becomes the mandatory EXIT GATE of the deployment stage (using existing gate machinery), and its kinds split cleanly — X/Y axis → Conservation Win (conservation outcome kinds, zero money columns, stays in GEOCON); Z axis → Commercial Output (the only thing that can reach the Exchange).

EXCHANGE + BRIDGE + DEAL BOARD / CAMBIUM / DIRECTORY (`open_bridge_opportunity`, `bridge_opportunities`, `TokenDealRoom`, one-directional read-only citation, zero money columns in conservation) → UNCHANGED, firewall intact and in fact strengthened (the auto-drafted Success Story is a money-blind projection of receipts, exactly like the `get_chain_receipt` allowlist, and must pass `evidence_json_is_clean` before listing). New only: a manager-visible Carry lane replaces the admin-only door with a transparent status track.

WORKSPACE (`get_my_programs`) + WATCHLIST (`toggle_watch` / `get_my_watchlist`, a clean polymorphic kind/entity store) → these BECOME the Book and the Tape; a species watch-row is the seed of a Watch position; the five disjoint panels unify into one page.

NEW PERSISTENCE IS MINIMAL AND ADDITIVE: one thin `positions` join (researcher, species_id, axis, conviction, status, optional question text, optional program_id) plus one Tape VIEW over existing event tables. The position table holds NO money and NO evidence of its own (its band is derived from existing receipts/tics — graft of #3's hard rule that strength/ledger are VIEWS, never hand-maintained, so it can't drift out of sync). Therefore it cannot contaminate the firewall, it cannot fabricate (a position is intent until receipts land; the [EKLE:]/no-auto-save-AI-fact discipline holds), and the whole thing is reversible behind a feature flag.

- Portfolio-first inversion is real, not cosmetic: you take positions BEFORE you organize, you choose work-areas (axes) as a human choice up front, and a program is a consequence of conviction — you never start at a fixed route. This is precisely the founder's reconception, implemented end-to-end.
- The shipped VERB stops being a standalone novelty. Every receipt now visibly raises a specific position's evidence band off 0 and posts to the Tape — the satisfaction loop the isolated receipt lacked. A terminal EXIT GATE at deployment forces a single typed return — Conservation Win (X/Y) or Commercial Output (Z) — using the existing outcome ladder and gate machinery. - The Exchange becomes visible to the researcher as a Carry lane on their own Book (declared → endorsed → venn-verified → listed → deal room open), replacing the admin-only black-box door — without loosening the firewall.
- The conviction dial vs evidence-equity split is a new, explicit data-integrity guard: subjective intent is shown but never credited or sorted on; only receipt-backed evidence ranks the Book.
- The dormant 279-node chain becomes legible as 'the menu of what a Value position can become,' surfaced only inside Z-positions — reviving a feared-wasted asset without reviving the rejected 6-stage vocabulary.
- The firewall is reframed from constraint to credibility engine: a Book's currency is evidence, not cash, which is exactly what makes it trustable to an institution or VC on sight.

## Hardest truths
- The hard part is NOT the build — it's the felt experience, which is the one thing you cannot verify with `npm run build`. The whole bet is that a Book of three positions feels momentous rather than lonely at one user. If the cards, the band-lift animation, and the Tape don't deliver a genuine sense of motion, this is just the watchlist with extra steps. This must be judged by the founder's gut on the real thing, not specced.
- This visibly resembles the reverted personalization/bench arc (3-axis personas, claim-species). The defense is real but must be held with discipline: it adds NO new claim mechanic and NO new evidence substrate — a position only GROUPS already-shipped receipts under a species×axis and renames engine concepts. If it grows a single bespoke 'claim' or hand-maintained strength column, it becomes the rejected arc again and should be reverted.
- The forced exit gate is the sharpest double edge. It is the founder's explicit demand (a program MUST become a conservation OR commercial output) and it is also coercive for genuinely open-ended basic research. A published method / banked accession must be a fully first-class, equally celebrated Conservation Win, and there must be an honest 'still exploring / parked' state, or the gate punishes good science and pressures researchers to over-claim a commercial output just to close — which would corrode the data-integrity ethos the whole platform rests on.
- The metaphor leaks toward money. 'Book / position / conviction / carry / exit' is fund language; on conservation data that risks inviting money expectations the firewall forbids. The mitigation is structural, not verbal: the currency is the 0–1 evidence band, X/Y positions can NEVER list on the Exchange, and the money-blind CHECK + receipt allowlist stay the enforcers. But the founder must consciously accept fund-metaphor language on a conservation product, or pick plainer Turkish words up front.
- The auto-drafted Success Story is the one genuinely new place data projects outward, and an LLM drafting from a ledger can smuggle a money/PII phrase into a conservation-side artifact. It MUST route through the existing evidence_json_is_clean / allowlist discipline and get a /crosscheck before it can list. This is the only part of the synthesis that deserves the second-model review every time.
- Cold-start honesty cuts both ways: making the Exchange/Carry visible to a lone researcher raises an expectation that a cold market (~0 paid members, ~0 funds) will not meet for a long time. The Carry lane must never fabricate demand signals ('2 funds viewing') when there are none — honesty about an empty market is the same discipline as never fabricating a data value, or it erodes trust identically.

## Open questions (founder call)
- Vocabulary in Turkish — does 'Book / position / conviction / exit' land in plain Turkish without reading as betting/finance, or do we want 'üstlenilen iş / pozisyon / kanaat' or even retire the fund words entirely for botanical-plain language? This is a taste + tone call only the founder can make, and it determines whether the firewall stays conceptually clean.
- Should a position be PUBLIC as an open question by default (maximizing the North-Star 'the gap is the product' and recruitment), or private-first? Broadcasting species × question × locality is a scoop/IP risk for the researcher and a sensitivity risk for threatened plants. Needs the founder's call on default visibility + an opt-in 'hold privately first' mode, tiered like locality data.
- How hard is the forced exit? Is it a true gate a program cannot pass without declaring an output, or a strong nudge with an honest 'parked / still exploring' escape? This is the line between 'organized, accountable work' and 'coercing open-ended science' — a strategy call, not a technical one.
- Conviction dial — keep it as a visible subjective signal that never sorts/credits the Book, or cut it entirely to avoid any vanity-inflation risk to the data-integrity ethos? (My recommendation is keep-but-never-credit, but the founder owns the data-integrity line.)
- Does a Conservation Win get its OWN destination beyond the public Outcomes feed — e.g., a steward/funding call or an IUCN-citable channel — so conservation success has a 'market' of recognition and support that is not the commercial Exchange? This decides whether X/Y work feels equally rewarded or quietly second-class.
- Naming the whole thing for the founder: is this 'The Book' (fund framing), 'The Field' (#3's 47,000-unkept-plants framing), or something Turkish and botanical? The chosen name sets the entire felt experience and should be his.
