# v-next — The Gear Machine (the systematic + story, inferred from real example outputs)

> 6 domain-gears grounded in real geophyte science (Galanthus, Colchicum, Crocus, Drimia, Narcissus, Pancratium) + a synthesis. The program systematic the founder felt was weak, built bottom-up from concrete outputs. For the story/narrative, NOT equity.

## The machine
THE GEAR MACHINE is one species' research life rendered as a gear-train. Each GEAR is a domain of work (a work-area) on that one plant; a PROGRAM is a team turning a gear; the SPECIES PAGE is the gearbox housing that holds them all and never empties. Gears do not float — they MESH through real scientific prerequisites: one gear's settled output is the tooth that lets the next gear turn. The findings resolve to seven gears in two layers.

FOUNDATIONAL LAYER (the load-bearing bottom, X-safeguard + Y-knowledge; outputs are terminal goods, never feedstock-for-money):
1. IDENTITY / TAXONOMY (Y, root gear) — fixes WHAT the plant is. Turns first, cold-start, fed only by the outside world. Hands forward a settled name + voucher/type + authenticated identity. Nothing downstream is valid without it (a wrong name attaches real chemistry to the wrong plant — proven: ~20% of traded medicinal drugs adulterated, ~6% with different toxic properties).
2. SAFEGUARD / CONSERVATION (X) — fixes WHETHER the plant survives. Outputs ARE the value (a filed Red List assessment, a banked accession, a stabilized population). Its cold-start fuel is the gap itself: only 3,267 of 47,413 atlas species carry a real IUCN status. It is the GATING TOOTH on the value layer: the value gear physically cannot mesh until X has delivered "this molecule can be supplied without taking the last wild bulbs."
3. PROPAGATION / CULTIVATION (X+Y, the bridge gear) — turns a wild threatened plant into renewable, documented MATERIAL + the method to remake it. Hands the value layer its physical precondition: a renewable, identity-confirmed, compound-bearing tissue line; hands the safeguard gear its ex-situ accessions and its honest "cannot-bank, route to cryo" verdicts.
4. CHEMISTRY / EXTRACTION (Z-feeding, still foundational) — turns material into molecule-level FACTS (provenance → method → yield % → phytochemical fingerprint → isolated compound + structure → quantitation vs a standard). This is the structural BRIDGE: a conservation program may stop here (a profile is knowledge, Y); a value program cannot proceed without it. It hands UP the single fact a value claim rests on, and feeds BACK to safeguard (galantamine's ~50% recovery cap and pancratistatin's near-zero wild yield are conservation arguments quantified).

VALUE LAYER (the upper gear, Z; engages only when fed; money-blind — produces a documented POTENTIAL, never a product/price):
5. BIOACTIVITY / PHARMACOLOGY (Z, the value gear) — takes a settled compound and asks "what does it DO to a named target, how strongly?" (IC50/EC50 + target + mechanism + translation signal). This is where the FAN FIRES: the instant a compound is named, every public ChEMBL/PubMed compound→target→indication edge flips visible at honest tier T1 ("the molecule does X; this accession is untested for X"). It hands a money-blind COMMERCIAL POTENTIAL RECORD up to the Exchange, which may only CITE it one-directional.

BRIDGE GEAR (sits between, carries the consent gate):
6. ETHNOBOTANY / TRADITIONAL USE (Y→Z, PIC/MAT-gated) — documents human use-knowledge as evidenced fact, and where consent is recorded, hands a candidate indication UP to chemistry/bioactivity. Fail-closed: without documented PIC/MAT it may turn for Y (cite the literature) but is mechanically LOCKED from feeding Z (the anti-biopiracy tooth). A documented traditional use is the single strongest predictor of a real value lead (squill→asthma RCT; colchicum→gout→FDA colchicine; snowdrop folk use→galantamine).

HOW THEY INTERLOCK (one machine, not six tools): the meshing is NOT a fixed pipeline — it is a dependency web of "this output is the precondition for that work." Identity is the bottom gear every other gear keys to. Safeguard and Propagation sit on Identity and feed each other (you cannot bank what is not named; you cannot propagate what is not assessed-as-worth-it). Chemistry sits on Propagation (renewable, vouchered material) and Identity (the label on every number). Bioactivity sits on Chemistry (an identified compound) and Material (an authenticated lineage) and STALLS legibly until both are fed — that stall is itself a signal. Ethnobotany can short-circuit and point chemistry at the right organ. The whole train runs on the same five-primitive tic-grammar (the locked tic-architecture); a gear is just a region of that one shared graph, and "interlock" is the `requires` edge between tics in different regions.

## Gear table
- **Identity / Taxonomy** [foundational] — kapsar: Fixes what the plant IS: nomenclature & typification, synonymy & circumscription, formal new-species description, morphological + molecular/cytogenetic characterization, vouchering & barcode authentication. Manufactures the settled name everything else bolts onto. · besler: EVERY other gear (it is the root). Hands forward: a settled accepted name + deposited type/voucher + authenticated identity (optionally a barcode + chromosome count). Safeguard cannot file a Red List assessment, Chemistry cannot label a compound, Propagation cannot accession, Value cannot key a receipt-lineage to species_id without it.
- **Safeguard / Conservation** [foundational] — kapsar: Fixes whether the plant survives: threat & risk assessment (IUCN/CITES/national), population baseline & demography, distribution & range, ex-situ securing (seed bank/cryo/tissue culture), reintroduction & long-term monitoring, habitat & threat-abatement (incl. propagation-for-trade as a pressure valve). Its outputs ARE the value, never feedstock. · besler: The VALUE gear (Z) and Knowledge (Y) and the species page. It is the gating tooth: Z cannot mesh until X hands up a secured, non-wild supply (banked accession + working ex-situ protocol + a CITES 'artificially propagated' pathway — the Galanthus village-propagation model). Without that, a Z potential is a driver of extinction, not value.
- **Propagation / Cultivation** [foundational] — kapsar: Turns a wild threatened plant into renewable, documented MATERIAL + the method to remake it: seed biology/dormancy, seed-storage behaviour (orthodox vs recalcitrant = the go/no-go for banking), tissue culture/micropropagation, cryopreservation, horticultural domestication, living-collection/ex-situ accession (provenance + barcode). · besler: UP to Chemistry/Bioactivity (renewable, identity-confirmed, compound-bearing tissue + the protocol — you cannot isolate pancratistatin or measure galantamine AChE inhibition without material that did not come from digging the last wild bulbs). DOWN/sideways to Safeguard (settles accessions, the recalcitrant 'cannot-bank' verdict, the cultivation-vs-wild-harvest case). To Knowledge (every protocol is a citable method).
- **Chemistry / Extraction** [foundational] — kapsar: Turns material into molecule-level FACTS: provenance/voucher of the chemical sample (organ, population, phenophase), extraction method + parameters, yield as a hard number, full phytochemical fingerprint, compound isolation to purity + structure (NMR/HRMS/X-ray), quantitation vs a reference standard. Records honest gaps (no standard, no validated assay, wild yield too low to supply) as first-class outputs. · besler: UP to Bioactivity (a purity-defined, structurally-confirmed, quantified compound — an IC50 is uninterpretable without it) and to the Z/value layer (the single fact a commercial potential rests on: 'this organ of this population yields X% of compound Y by this validated method'). BACK to Safeguard (yield-per-biomass + recovery caps quantify wild-collection pressure).
- **Ethnobotany / Traditional Use** [value] — kapsar: Documents human traditional/local use-knowledge as evidenced fact (primary field surveys with use-report/ICF/fidelity metrics; historical reconstruction from Dioscorides/Avicenna/Unani/Ayurveda/TCM; consent provenance under Nagoya PIC/MAT; bridge-framing a use into a testable candidate). Records the honest BLANK where no use exists (Sternbergia, Pancratium). · besler: UP, money-blind and consent-gated, a prioritized candidate hypothesis to Chemistry ('this use implicates organ X — isolate the constituent'), Bioactivity ('squill for asthma', 'saffron for depression'), and the Z gear ONLY through the PIC/MAT gate (fail-closed: no consent → locked from feeding Z, but may still feed Y by citing the literature).
- **Bioactivity / Pharmacology** [value] — kapsar: Takes a settled compound + authenticated lineage and characterizes what it DOES: bioassay/screening against a named target, potency (IC50/EC50/Ki + pChEMBL), mechanism/target, selectivity/ADMET flags, the money-blind commercial-potential record (compound→target→mechanism→indication-area + confidence band), and translation signals (trial existence/phase/approved drug). Marks honest gaps (pancratistatin, lycorine: real literature, no standardized assay record) as valid outputs. · besler: UP into the EXCHANGE/Venture (the only place a product/price exists; it may only CITE this record, read-only, one-directional). Into the species-page aggregator (each characterized activity becomes a standing fact). Into conservation prioritisation (raising the documented stakes of losing a wild population that carries an active compound).
- **Species page (the gearbox / aggregator)** [foundational] — kapsar: Not a gear that is turned — the durable HOUSING that holds all gears for one plant. Accumulates every program, output, receipt, evidence band and potential forever; outlives any researcher or program. One per plant = the atlas page. · besler: Nothing forward — it is the terminal sink and the public face. Every gear's settled output accrues here permanently, raising the species' evidence band off 0. It is what 'one place per plant' means in the locked model — the species, not a mega-program.

## How a program starts (and turns)
A program starts as a POSITION and becomes a turning gear in five plain steps — traced on a real geophyte, Galanthus elwesii.

(1) TAKE THE POSITION (free, zero-ceremony, evidence band = 0). A researcher opens a card phrased as the open question he actually owns: 'Sevket — is the snowdrop population I collected in NE Anatolia G. alpinus or the segregate G. koenenianus?' That is a faint Watch position on Galanthus × the Identity gear. It costs nothing and commits to nothing; it is public AS A QUESTION (the gap is now visibly his).

(2) TURN THE GEAR WITH THE VERB (band lifts off 0; Watch→Active). He asserts one DOI-backed fact and mints a money-blind receipt — the lightest evidence first: deposit a voucher with locality + collector, then a diagnostic character, then a barcode (rbcL/matK/ITS2) placing it in the Turkey-focused phylogeny that confirmed G. koenenianus as distinct (Taşci Margoz et al. 2013). Each receipt is a tooth cut into the Identity gear; the band ticks weak→moderate. The Tape records the first line.

(3) PROMOTE TO A MANAGED PROGRAM (the gear is now actively driven). When conviction builds, 'Manage this position' calls create_program pre-filled (species set, axis→region, question→rationale, receipts attached as opening evidence). The program runs the unchanged Venn engine inside the position; its tailored bench is the Identity work-environment (synonymy table, voucher field, character matrix, barcode/karyotype slots). Terminal Identity moves — designate a type, publish synonymy, validly describe a new taxon — are the gear's strongest teeth.

(4) THE SETTLED OUTPUT MESHES THE NEXT GEAR. The settled name is the tooth that lets adjacent gears turn. Now a SECOND program can open on the SAME plant, a different gear, by a different (or the same) researcher: Galanthus elwesii × Safeguard. Its first tic cites the CITES Appendix-II listing + the village artificial-propagation alternative; the output is a Conservation Win (a banked, non-wild supply), which is the gating tooth the Value gear needs. A THIRD program opens Galanthus elwesii × Value: one tic — sci.metabolite_profiling — names GALANTAMINE + LYCORINE, and THE FAN FIRES: dozens of money-blind potentials flip visible at T1 (galantamine→AChE→Alzheimer, flagged with the live approved-drug precedent CHEMBL659→CHEMBL220, max_phase 4; lycorine→antiproliferative/antiviral, all honestly T1-untested-on-this-accession). Enrichment tics promote ONE point: crude screen T1→T2, isolation+pure-potency T2→T3, mechanism T3→T4.

(5) THE OUTPUT, NEVER 'DONE'. The Identity program may sit passive as a standing open call another researcher revives. The Safeguard program's banked accession sits as a revivable safeguard. The Value program declares, at deployment, a Commercial Output = 'documented acetylcholinesterase-inhibition potential, G. elwesii, T3/verified, provenance DOI' — a POTENTIAL, money-blind, that climbs self→peer→org→venn and is the only thing the Exchange may cite. Three parallel programs, three gears, one plant, all rolling up to the Galanthus elwesii species page forever. Where the honest output is a gap (no in-vitro protocol exists specifically for G. elwesii — the galantamine tissue-culture work is on Narcissus/Leucojum relatives), the program states the gap as the work, never fabricates a yield.

## The story
Here is the story the founder felt was missing — the felt narrative of the machine, in plain language.

A plant is a small engine waiting to be built. On day one its gearbox — the species page — is almost empty: a name, maybe a photo, and 47,000 plants like it with nothing kept. That emptiness is not the failure; it is the invitation. 'This plant is unkept. What do you want to fix about it?'

You pick a gear. Not a route, not a wizard — a gear, a domain of real work: what it IS (taxonomy), whether it SURVIVES (conservation), how to GROW it (propagation), what it's MADE of (chemistry), what it DOES to a body (bioactivity), how people have USED it (ethnobotany). You phrase your gear as the open question you actually care about, with your name on it. That question is now yours and the world can see you own that gap.

Then you turn the gear — one DOI-backed fact at a time. Every fact is a tooth cut into the gear, and the gear visibly thickens: the evidence bar climbs off zero, weak → moderate → strong → verified. This is the satisfaction the platform lacked: not points, not confetti — the sober, compounding sense of a real thing being built, fact by cited fact, in a field notebook voice.

The magic is the MESHING. You do not build the whole machine alone, and you do not build it in a fixed line. The moment you settle a gear, its output becomes the tooth that lets the NEXT gear turn — maybe turned by someone else entirely. You fix the snowdrop's name; a conservationist a continent away can now bank it; once it's banked and renewable, a chemist can profile it without touching a wild bulb; the instant the chemist names galantamine, a hundred money-blind potentials light up across medicine, nutrition, cosmetics — each honestly labelled 'the molecule does this; THIS plant is untested.' No one person did it all. 'What one could not, another can.' The gears are independent domains, but they pull each other forward — that is the interlock the founder kept reaching for.

And there is a hard floor in the machine that makes it trustworthy: the value gear at the top — the commercial one — physically cannot turn until the foundational gears below it have settled. You cannot reveal a plant's commercial potential until you have first proven it can be supplied WITHOUT taking the last wild ones. Conservation is not a tax on value; it is the tooth value needs. That is why a book of these plants is trustable on sight: its currency is evidence, never cash, and the wall between them is not a rule someone enforces — it is the shape of the gearbox. There is nowhere to put money. The whole machine runs forward, gear by gear, plant by plant, and every tooth cut stays in the gearbox forever, long after any one researcher walks away.

## Worked example
GALANTHUS ELWESII (giant snowdrop), traced gear-by-gear through its REAL outputs — the science confirmed live in ChEMBL/Consensus this session.

GEAR 1 — IDENTITY (Y, foundational, turns first). Output: a genus-wide molecular phylogeny (nrITS + 4 plastid regions) that resolves 7 clades and shows Galanthus is monophyletic BUT EMBEDDED IN LEUCOJUM, flagging taxa for recircumscription (Rønsted et al. 2013; Lledó et al. 2004); a Turkey-focused phylogeny confirms G. koenenianus distinct from G. alpinus (Taşci Margoz et al. 2013). SETTLED OUTPUT HANDED UP: the accepted name + voucher + barcode — the label every later number bolts onto.

GEAR 2 — SAFEGUARD (X, foundational, meshes on Identity). Output: a CITES Appendix-II export-quota regime with annual field inspections since the late 1980s (G. elwesii is Turkey's most important wild-collected export flower bulb — Zeybekoğlu 2024; Read 1989), PLUS the proven non-wild alternative — a 10-year, 3-village, 250+-villager artificial-propagation project producing bulbs meeting CITES 'artificially propagated' criteria (Entwistle et al. 2002). SETTLED OUTPUT (the gating tooth): a secured supply that does NOT depend on wild collection — exactly what the Value gear needs before it may turn.

GEAR 3 — PROPAGATION (X+Y, the bridge; honest gap on this exact species). HONEST OUTPUT: no published in-vitro protocol exists specifically for G. elwesii — the galantamine tissue-culture work is on Narcissus/Leucojum relatives (twin-scale → up to 31 bulblets/explant; regenerated bulblets HPLC-MS-assayed at 40 µg/gDW galantamine in light vs 20 in dark; field basal-plate tissue still out-yields in vitro — Rahimi Khonakdari 2020; Ferdausi 2020). The gear states the gap as the work; it does NOT fabricate a G. elwesii yield.

GEAR 4 — CHEMISTRY (Z-feeding, foundational). Output: validated HPLC/HPLC-DAD quantitation of galantamine (+ co-occurring lycorine) from bulbs/aerial parts, a green ionic-liquid extraction removing harmful solvents, AND the load-bearing recovery-cap fact: classical solvent extraction recovers only ~50% of the galantamine present (Akram 2020; Svinyarov 2016; Emir 2016). That ~50% cap is simultaneously a chemistry fact and a conservation argument (it tells you how much wild biomass a unit of drug costs). SETTLED OUTPUT HANDED UP: an identified, quantified compound + a reference standard.

GEAR 5 — BIOACTIVITY (Z, the value gear, fan fires). Output: galantamine is an acetylcholinesterase (AChE) INHIBITOR — human AChE IC50 clustering ~0.5–0.8 µM across 87 records (pChEMBL 6.1–7.0); mechanism mec_id 741 → target CHEMBL220, INHIBITOR, direct interaction, disease_efficacy true; FULLY TRANSLATED — approved drug since 2001 (CHEMBL659, ATC N06DA04, anti-Alzheimer's), with benzgalantamine (2024) layered on. The money-blind potential record reads honestly two-axis: 'molecule: verified anti-Alzheimer; THIS accession: not yet assayed.' Lycorine inherits the same fan as T1 (real literature, no structured assay record under that name — a flagged gap). EXIT: a Commercial Output = 'documented AChE-inhibition potential, G. elwesii, T-verified, provenance DOI' — a POTENTIAL, money-blind, climbing self→peer→org→venn; the Exchange may only cite it. The galantamine drug/deal NEVER appears in GEOCON.

ALL FIVE roll up permanently to the Galanthus elwesii species page — the gearbox — raising its evidence band off 0 forever.

## Reconciliation with the locked model
The gear machine adds NO new mechanism — it is a re-narration of the LOCKED MODEL and the tic-architecture, term-for-term, which is the whole defence against the v1 complexity death.

- SPECIES = THE MACHINE/GEARBOX. The locked 'species is the durable aggregator' IS the gearbox housing: one per plant, accumulates all gears/programs/outputs/potentials forever, outlives any researcher. A gear is never 'one place per plant'; the species is.
- A PROGRAM = WORK TURNING A GEAR. The locked 'program = work on a domain for a species, a living value engine, active/passive/handoff, multiple parallel per plant' IS a gear being turned. A gear is exactly a work-area; turning it is the program; a passive gear is a standing open call another researcher revives ('what one could not, another can'). Multiple parallel programs per plant = multiple gears (and multiple programs on the SAME gear) turning at once. A mixed-area team = one program meshing teeth across gears.
- THE VERB FEEDS EVIDENCE. Each tooth cut into a gear is one DOI-backed fact → money-blind receipt, lifting the 0–1 band. No gear thickens by clicking; only the verb cuts teeth. Conviction stays separate and never credited — unchanged.
- THE FIREWALL IS THE SHAPE OF THE GEARBOX, NOT A RULE. The foundational layer (Identity/Safeguard/Propagation/Chemistry, X/Y) physically sits below the value gear (Bioactivity, Z), which 'only engages when fed by settled foundational outputs' — this is literally the tic-architecture's unlock rule (clause 4: X/Y positions can only see conservation_only terminals; Z positions only translational). There is nowhere to put money: chain_evidence.value_json carries no money key (evidence_json_is_clean CHECK), the receipt projects an allowlist, the product/price lives ONLY in the Exchange citing GEOCON one-directional. Conservation IS value (X/Y outputs are terminal goods); Z is a money-blind POTENTIAL, never a product.
- EQUITY AT Z, settled separately (NOT analyzed here, per instruction). Equity is born only at a Z/commercial output and flows through the receipt/evidence lineage in the Exchange — the gear machine changes none of this.
- THE GEAR METAPHOR = THE INTERLOCK ALREADY IN THE SUBSTRATE. 'Gears interlock, one turning helps the next' is exactly the tic-architecture's `requires` / prereq edges between tics in different regions (taxonomy_verified before everything; metabolite_profiling before the fan). The gear machine is therefore a LEGIBILITY SKIN over the shipped engine + the 279-chain — it resurrects no rejected 6-stage vocabulary, renames no DB enum, and adds nothing load-bearing. It is the founder's felt 'systematic + story' laid faithfully over parts already designed.

## Open questions (founder call)
- GEAR GRANULARITY (the taste call that sets everything): are these SEVEN gears the right cut, or do some collapse/split? Specifically — is Ethnobotany its own gear or a feeder lane into Chemistry/Bioactivity? Is Propagation one gear or two (seed-biology vs tissue-culture)? Too many gears re-creates the v1 complexity; too few hides the real interlocks. This is the founder's structural call and it determines how many work-area environments get built.
- HOW HARD IS THE VALUE-GEAR FLOOR? The model says the Z gear 'physically cannot turn until X/Propagation settle a non-wild supply.' Is that a TRUE mechanical lock (a Z program is blocked from starting until a Safeguard win exists on the same species) or a STRONG visible nudge (the fan fires but the potential is strength-capped near 0 until supply is settled)? A real lock is the cleanest firewall story but coerces; a cap-toward-0 is the tic-architecture's current stance. Founder must pick the gate hardness.
- DOES THE PROPAGATION/CHEMISTRY BRIDGE BELONG TO X OR Z? Chemistry produces a Y-knowledge fact (a profile) that is ALSO the precondition for a Z potential — the same gear feeds both a conservation argument (the ~50% galantamine recovery cap = wild-collection pressure) and the value fan. Is a chemistry program declared X/Y or Z up front, or can one gear legitimately carry both axes at once? The locked model pre-commits exit-type by axis, so a dual-axis bridge gear needs an explicit rule.
- HOW WIDE DOES THE T1 FAN BROADCAST? The instant a compound is named, dozens-to-hundreds of money-blind potentials flip visible. This is the North-Star payoff ('the gap is the product') AND a scoop/over-claim surface. Public to the world, or member-gated like locality data until T2+? A firewall-adjacent reputational call only the founder can make.
- WHO AUTHORS THE INTERLOCK (the gear-to-gear `requires` edges + the tic→chain welds)? It is small (tens of edges, mostly derivable from the chain's parent→child order) but it is where domain truth lives — a wrong edge mis-meshes the machine. Founder decides: seed-then-curate himself, delegate to a science advisor, or AI-draft with [EKLE:] placeholders for human sign-off.
- IS A CONSERVATION/KNOWLEDGE GEAR EVER 'FINISHED'? The model says programs are never done and a passive gear is a revivable open call. But a banked accession or a filed Red List assessment FEELS like a completed deliverable. Does the species page show a gear as 'standing/settled' (a finished tooth) distinct from 'passive/parked' (an open call), or is everything just active/passive? This decides whether finished good science reads as an achievement or as an unfinished task.
- DOES EVERY PLANT GET THE SAME SEVEN-GEAR TEMPLATE, OR ONLY THE GEARS THAT MAKE SENSE? A non-medicinal, data-poor stub geophyte may only ever have an Identity and a Safeguard gear. Does its species page show all seven gears as empty/locked (honest gap, maximizes North-Star) or only surface the gears with any evidence (cleaner, less daunting)? A cold-start UX call with a data-integrity edge.

## The six gears (grounded detail)
### Identity / Taxonomy — Y (knowledge) — foundational, NOT value-layer. It is the root gear of the whole machine: it is structurally below every 
**Kapsar:** The work and knowledge that FIX WHAT THE PLANT IS, so that every later claim attaches to a stable, traceable name. Concretely, a program working this gear produces: (1) NOMENCLATURE & TYPIFICATION — the correct accepted name, its authorship, and the physical type that anchors it: holotypes for new species, and lectotypes/neotypes/epitypes when an old name was never properly anchored (the Drimia 'undata group' work designated 3 lectotypes, 2 neotypes, 1 epitype and revived two names). (2) SYNONYMY & CIRCUMSCRIPTION — which published names are the same plant and which are real, i.e. drawing the species boundary: sinking inflated names to infraspecific rank (Narcissus sect. Bulbocodii — 7 'species' demoted under N. bulbocodium) or splitting/recircumscribing genera (Galanthus shown to be embedded in Leucojum; Drimia split from Urginea/Charybdis). (3) FORMAL SPECIES DESCRIPTION — the validly published protologue of a new taxon with a Latin/English diagnosis, diagnostic characters vs. nearest relatives, and a designated type (Colchicum leptanthum; the six Turkish Colchicum of Persson 1999). (4) MORPHOLOGICAL CHARACTERIZATION — the diagnostic character matrix and identification key that let anyone else re-identify the plant. (5) MOLECULAR/CYTOGENETIC CHARACTERIZATION — phylogenies (nrITS, matK, trnL-F, ndhF), chromosome counts/karyotypes (2n=18 in C. leptanthum), DNA barcodes (rbcL/matK/ITS2), and genome-level origin resolution (saffron = autotriploid of Crocus cartwrightianus). (6) VOUCHERING & AUTHENTICATION — a deposited, cited herbarium specimen for every downstream claim, and barcode-based detection of misidentified/adulterated material. The scope is NOT 'naming for its own sake' — it is manufacturing the one part everything else bolts onto.
**Beslenir:** Almost nothing inside the system — this is the bottom gear, the one that must turn first and can turn cold-start with no upstream output. Its real 'feed' is EXTERNAL raw material: field-collected specimens with locality + collector data, existing herbarium/type material (often historical and in need of lectotypification), published protologues and prior synonymy to reconcile, and the molecular/cytogenetic data it generates itself (sequences, karyotypes, barcodes). The only weak internal back-feed is a CORRECTION loop: when a downstream gear hits an anomaly (e.g. chemistry finds a population whose alkaloid profile doesn't match the named species, or a barcode flags a mismatch), that anomaly can re-engage this gear to re-circumscribe or describe a cryptic taxon — so Identity is fed-by-default by the outside world, and fed only secondarily by downstream surprises that force a re-identification. · **Besler:** EVERY other gear — it is the root, and the thing it hands forward is a SETTLED NAME + a deposited voucher/type + an authenticated identity (optionally a barcode and chromosome count). Nothing downstream is valid without it: (1) Safeguard/Conservation (X) cannot file an IUCN Red-List assessment, define a range, or bank an ex-situ accession for a taxon whose boundary isn't drawn — a newly described narrow-endemic Colchicum is only assessable once it has a name and type. (2) Chemistry/Extraction and Bioactivity (Y/Z) inherit the name as the LABEL on every compound, IC50, and assay — the barcode study proves that a wrong name attaches real chemistry to the wrong plant (6% of traded drugs had different toxic properties). (3) Propagation/Cultivation needs the exact taxon and its wild progenitor (saffron work tells a propagation program its source is C. cartwrightianus). (4) Value (Z) potentials and the Exchange receipt lineage are keyed to species_id — the firewall's benefit-sharing trace is only fair if the identity is correct. In engine terms it is the literal precondition of the foundation stage: the first tic of almost any program is 'is the identity fixed and vouchered?', and the identity output is what lets a stage gate open.
**Program nasıl başlar:** A researcher opens a position on a species × the Identity/Taxonomy work-area, phrased as the real open question they care about — e.g. 'Sevket — is the snowdrop population I collected in NE Anatolia G. alpinus or the segregate G. koenenianus?', or 'Are the seven Bulbocodii daffodil names real species or inflation?', or 'What is the wild progenitor of the saffron clone, and does my accession match it?'. ### Ethnobotany / Traditional Use (PIC/MAT consent-gated) — Mixed Y→Z, value-layer (a bridge gear). Primary face is Y (knowledge: it documents human use-knowledge as evidenced fact
**Kapsar:** Documents and provenances HUMAN traditional/local use-knowledge of a geophyte as an evidenced fact, and where consented, hands a candidate "potential" up to the value gear. Concretely it covers four kinds of work: (1) Primary-field documentation — ethnobotanical surveys that record who uses the plant, for what ailment, which organ, which preparation, with quantitative rigor metrics (use-report count, frequency-of-citation, Informant Consensus Factor, Fidelity Level), e.g. NE-Turkey/Mersin surveys logging geophytes in folk medicine [Karaköse 2022; Emre 2021]. (2) Literature/historical reconstruction — tracing a documented use back through written corpora (Dioscorides, Avicenna's Canon, Unani/Ayurveda/TPM texts, Ebers-era records) into a citable lineage, e.g. Colchicum gout use from Babylonia → Alexander of Tralles → FDA colchicine [Akbar 2020; Alusik 2019]; snowdrop folk use that prefigured galantamine [Cozanitis 2021]; saffron-as-antidepressant in Avicenna [Xing 2021]. (3) Consent provenance — recording Prior Informed Consent (PIC) and Mutually Agreed Terms (MAT) under the Nagoya Protocol/CBD ABS regime, naming the knowledge-holder community and the benefit-sharing terms; this is the gate, and its absence (biopiracy) is the gear's defining failure mode [Bajpai 2025; Kariyawasam 2018]. (4) Bridge-framing — turning a documented use into a testable, money-blind candidate hypothesis ("Squill Oxymel for asthma", "Bedouin anticancer squill use") that the chemistry/bioactivity gears can act on. It does NOT cover: assay numbers (Bioactivity gear), isolation/yield (Chemistry gear), conservation status (Safeguard gear), or any product/price (Exchange — firewalled out).
**Beslenir:** Engages when fed by: (1) Taxonomy/Identification gear — a confirmed species identity (the exact geophyte the use attaches to; mis-ID poisons the whole chain, so this must settle first). (2) Safeguard/Conservation gear (X) — IUCN status + wild-harvest pressure, because a CR/EN wild-collected geophyte changes whether traditional wild-harvest can even be documented as ongoing vs. must be flagged as a conservation conflict (many anchor geophytes are threatened by wild collection). (3) The external knowledge corpus itself — primary informants (survey respondents, herbalists) and the historical/written record (Dioscorides, Avicenna, Unani/TPM/Ayurveda texts, prior ethnobotanical surveys). Critically, the consent sub-gate is fed by the legal/ABS layer (Nagoya jurisdiction status, community governance) — without that input the gear cannot turn its forward (Z) face at all. · **Besler:** Hands UP, money-blind, a prioritized, consent-stamped candidate hypothesis to: (1) Chemistry/Phytochemistry gear — "this documented use implicates organ X / preparation Y; isolate the responsible constituent" (e.g. squill use → proscillaridin A isolation; colchicum gout use → colchicine). (2) Bioactivity/Pharmacology gear — a testable indication hypothesis ("squill for asthma", "saffron for depression", "snowdrop for cognition") that the assay/target gears validate. (3) The Value (Z) gear — but ONLY through the consent gate: a documented use becomes a fed-forward "potential" solely when PIC/MAT is recorded; otherwise the interlock to Z is mechanically disengaged (fail-closed).
