# Venn Exchange — The Cambium (venture-lifecycle layer, design)

> 4-agent design workflow (3 creative concepts + creative-director synthesis). The chosen concept. DESIGN — build v1 next.

## Name
The Cambium

## One-liner
A venture-lifecycle layer that renders each VENN venture as the cross-section of a living stem — a frozen-evidence green pith at the centre, one growth ring deposited per funding round, a breathing "cambium" edge for the live stage, and a bark/bloom skin for the off-ramp — so you read a venture's whole journey the way a dendrologist reads a trunk: outward, ring by ring, and the rings cannot lie.

## Visual metaphor
A botanical stem/bulb cross-section (dendrochronology). The cambium is the thin living layer just under the bark that lays down exactly one ring per growing season; every inner ring is a finished, dated, immutable record. Mapped to a venture: radius = funding stage (distance from centre), angle = elapsed time within a stage (a ring sweeps clockwise from 12 o'clock and the sweep arc is proportional to dwell time), ring thickness = round magnitude relative to the venture's own history (never an absolute money figure), the cambium = the live outermost in-progress ring (the only animated element — a slow "breathing" pulse that freezes grey when stalled), and the bark/skin encodes how a venture left the journey. This is deliberately the INVERSE topology of a kanban/donut: in a donut, angle is category and you drag cards between angular slices; here radius is stage and angle is time, growth is monotonic, and you cannot un-grow a ring — which is the append-only event log made physical. It is Venn-native (concentric rings echo the X/Y/Z program-engine Venn diagram) and mission-native (a bulb/stem cross-section for a geophyte atlas). The green pith is literally a different colour family (conservation green #1D9E75) from every commerce ring outward (amber #FFD15C/#F5A623/#E5722B on the violet #150821->#2a1240 cosmos), so the firewall is visible in the geometry: value grows OUTWARD from a green evidence seed and can never grow back inward.

## Radial structure
SVG, viewBox "0 0 720 720", centre c=(360,360). Single venture = one stem cross-section. Render order centre->edge: (1) PITH / evidence core, r=0->58: a solid disc filled conservation green #1D9E75 at ~0.9 opacity with a thin brighter ring; inside it a tiny three-lobe Venn glyph (X #1D9E75 / Y #185FA5 / Z #BA7517) and ONE number = endorsement/verification-tier count read from the frozen provenance_snapshot; a faint frozen_at date curves along its lower edge on a textPath. Pith never moves, never animates, never changes size. (2) HEARTWOOD RINGS, r=58->300: one concentric annulus per COMPLETED stage, drawn as an SVG arc/annulus path via a polarToCartesian + describeAnnularArc helper (standard donut-arc math: outer arc clockwise, inner arc counter-clockwise, two radial caps). Stage->ring radius bands are fixed (see stages). Ring is NOT always a full 360deg: its sweep angle = clamp( dwell_time_in_that_stage / reference_span ) mapped to [40deg .. 350deg], swept clockwise from 12 o'clock (-90deg). A fast round = short fat arc near the top; a dragged round = a long thin arc wrapping most of the way round and never closing. Ring STROKE WIDTH = round magnitude band (xs..xl -> 3..16px), so a bigger round lays down a fatter ring. Ring FILL/colour = the venture's originating-vertical brand_color, older rings deeper/desaturated, newer rings warmer/brighter (growth-toward-light gradient on the amber ramp). Ring fill DENSITY/stipple = evidence strength backing that round (sparse = thin diligence). (3) CAMBIUM, the live edge: the single outermost in-progress ring drawn at full saturation with a slow opacity pulse 0.6<->1.0 over ~3s, and at its leading clockwise tip a small bright "growth node" whose angle == current time-in-stage. When stalled the node desaturates to grey-amber #9b8c74 and the pulse halts (the wood goes dormant). (4) VASCULAR RAYS (investors): each cap-table investor = a thin radial line from pith outward to the ring where they entered, ending in a small dot; a follow-on investor's ray reaches further out (longer ray = longer relationship). Ray brightness scales with pipeline depth. (5) BARK / SKIN, r~300->340: the outermost stroke encodes the off-ramp (see off_ramps) — dashed grey halo (paused), a removed radial notch wedge (passed), a grafted second tiny pith at the rim (acqui-hire), or the ring opening into petals (exit bloom). PORTFOLIO VIEW ("the Grove"): the same component tiled, many small cross-sections on the violet field; sortable by stall/momentum/stage; conversion is rendered as ring-density per stage band aggregated across all ventures (a band many ventures passed through but few left glows denser — the bottleneck shows itself).

## Stages
- **idea** (Idea / formation) — Innermost ring band r=58->88. The venture exists as a venn_verified output that opened a door; faint hairline ring. Entry row of stage_history (from_stage=null).
- **pre_seed** (Pre-seed) — Ring band r=88->126. First external capital / friends-and-family. Thin ring. Stage-aware stall threshold is short (movement expected fast).
- **seed** (Seed) — Ring band r=126->172. Medium ring. The common stall point — the Grove deliberately makes a cluster stuck at the pre-seed->seed notch legible as a dense band.
- **series_a** (Series A) — Ring band r=172->216. Thick ring; product-market traction round. Longer expected dwell than seed.
- **series_b_plus** (Series B+) — Ring band r=216->256. Thick ring; collapses B, C, D into one outward band to avoid runaway radius at cold start (a single venture rarely needs B/C/D distinction in v1).
- **growth** (Growth) — Ring band r=256->300. Thick dense ring; late private capital / pre-exit scaling. Outermost heartwood before the bark.
- **exit** (Exit) — Terminal band r=300->340 rendered as the BARK/skin, not a heartwood ring. Reaching it triggers the bloom (IPO/acquisition) — the one earned celebratory state. The completed rings remain visible beneath as the receptacle, so the bloom is clearly the fruit of the history, not a reset.

## Off-ramps
- paused (resumable): a dashed grey bark ring just outside the last live ring; cambium pulse freezes in place. The wood is intact, the season simply stopped. event_type=pause, clears on resume.
- passed (line not pursued / we or the investor walked): a clean radial notch wedge removed from the cross-section, like a core sample taken and the tree left standing — honest, not punitive. event_type=pass; sets lifecycle_status='dead'.
- acqui_hire: the rings stop and a second tiny pith is grafted at the rim (a graft scar — the team's evidence carried into another stem). event_type=acquihire.
- exit_ipo / exit_acquisition (the bloom): the only state that intentionally breaks the wood metaphor — the outermost ring opens into petals over the dense ring-stack, a single ~800ms ease-out bloom. The completed rings remain as the receptacle beneath. This is the only white-gold highlight and the only celebratory motion; it exists ONLY when the ring history justifies it (no hype). event_type=exit_ipo|exit_acquisition; sets lifecycle_status='exited'.
- resume: not an off-ramp but the inverse of pause — re-lights the cambium and continues the live ring.

## Functional model
The funding lifecycle is a THIRD, venture-level axis layered over (not replacing) the two coarse trackers the bridge already has: bridge.opportunities.stage (idea/prototype/pilot/scaling = PRODUCT maturity) and bridge.opportunity_investor.pipeline_stage (matched->intro->nda->meeting->diligence->term_sheet->closed/passed = per-investor DEAL mechanics). The Cambium adds CAPITAL maturity. A venture can be 'pilot' product-wise but 'seed' capital-wise; pipeline_stage is this round's negotiation micro-state, the cambium ring is the venture's macro-position. A ring is DEPOSITED only by a verifiable, attributed transition event — never by dragging a card (there is no drag; the topology forbids sideways moves). Gate to advance from stage S to S+1: a round_close event for S+1 is recorded, AND the frozen provenance_snapshot is present and fresh (not past its freshness flag). Inward moves (down-round / re-stage) and off-ramps are always allowed from anywhere. Gates are advisory at cold start (the operator can override) but every override is itself a logged event — the instrument never silently rewrites history. The whole ring figure is therefore a pure PROJECTION of an append-only event log: you can never draw a ring that did not happen and never erase one. Each transition is recorded via an admin RPC (record_venture_event) that is SECURITY DEFINER, set search_path=public, calls _bridge_require_admin() first, EXECUTE-granted to authenticated (fails closed for non-admins), inserts one event row, and refreshes the denormalised current-stage cache.

## Data model
Additive, reversible (drop to revert), firewall-safe — everything in the isolated bridge schema (USAGE=service_role only; anon/authenticated false), conservation tables gain ZERO columns. (A) Two additive columns on bridge.opportunities: lifecycle_stage text NOT NULL DEFAULT 'idea' CHECK in (idea,pre_seed,seed,series_a,series_b_plus,growth,exit,paused,passed,acqui_hire,ipo,acquired); lifecycle_status text NOT NULL DEFAULT 'active' CHECK in (active,parked,exited,dead). These are a denormalised cache of the latest event. (B) The spine — a new append-only table bridge.venture_lifecycle_event: id uuid pk default gen_random_uuid(); opportunity_id uuid NOT NULL REFERENCES bridge.opportunities(id) ON DELETE CASCADE; seq int (monotonic per opportunity); event_type text CHECK in (round_open,round_close,pause,resume,pass,acquihire,exit_ipo,exit_acquisition,operator_override); from_stage text (null on the entry row); to_stage text (null for off-ramps); magnitude_band text CHECK in (xs,s,m,l,xl) — RELATIVE band ONLY, there is NO money column anywhere in this table; direction text CHECK in (outward,inward,offramp); investor_ids uuid[] (party to this event, references bridge.investors); evidence_ref jsonb {kind, ref_id, note} — a diligence/term-sheet pointer, money-free; occurred_at timestamptz NOT NULL (the timestamp that drives arc sweep + time-in-stage — distinct from when it was recorded); recorded_by uuid (profiles.id of the admin who logged it = 'who moved it / how it got there'); recorded_at timestamptz DEFAULT now(); note_md text. Index on (opportunity_id, seq) and (opportunity_id, occurred_at). Append-only: no UPDATE/DELETE in the normal path; a correction is a new compensating event (this is what makes 'you cannot un-grow a ring' TRUE, not just visual). (C) A small config table bridge.lifecycle_stage_config (stage text pk, expected_dwell_days int, stall_factor numeric, ring_inner_r int, ring_outer_r int) — seeds the stall thresholds and the radius bands so they are data, not fabricated magic numbers. (D) REUSE: the pith reads the existing frozen bridge.opportunities.provenance_snapshot; the rays read existing bridge.opportunity_investor (pipeline_stage drives ray brightness / the next-best-action engine); the 28-fund directory in bridge.investors supplies ray identities. No new investor or snapshot tables. Backfill: one entry event row (from_stage=null, to_stage=lifecycle_stage) per existing opportunity from its created_at, so every existing venture has a valid one-ring projection.

## Oversight computes
- time_in_current_stage = now() - occurred_at(last round_open). Drives the cambium growth-node angle.
- time_per_stage[] = the full per-ring dwell vector from consecutive event timestamps. Drives every ring's arc-sweep angle.
- stalled flag = time_in_current_stage > expected_dwell_days(stage) * stall_factor(stage), stage-AWARE (seed is allowed to idle longer than Series A; thresholds live in bridge.lifecycle_stage_config). Drives the grey, still cambium + a 'drifting' sort list.
- momentum = sign/slope of recent inter-event gaps (are rounds coming faster or slower than this venture's own history?). Drives cambium pulse rate / ring colour warmth.
- conversion / progression rate (portfolio level) = of all ventures that entered stage S, the fraction that reached S+1, plus median dwell per stage. Rendered as ring-DENSITY per stage band in the Grove — the bottleneck stage glows densest, no chart needed.
- evidence integrity = snapshot freshness vs its freshness flag, per venture. Drives pith luminosity; a stale-snapshot venture becomes a tracked oversight item ('re-cite before advancing').
- next_best_action = a DETERMINISTIC rule (not AI guesswork) over the projection + the existing opportunity_investor.pipeline_stage, surfaced as a one-line caption on the admin desk only. Examples: 'fresh snapshot, 14d in pre-seed -> ready to advance'; 'snapshot stale 90d -> re-cite before advancing'; 'stalled 8 months in seed; 3 funds at diligence, 0 at term_sheet -> nudge the warm intro, or decide pause/pass'. Never a money recommendation.

## Firewall notes
Commerce-side, bridge-only, one-directional — the Cambium TIGHTENS the firewall rather than testing it, because the metaphor itself enforces the rule. (1) Schema isolation intact: the two new columns + bridge.venture_lifecycle_event + bridge.lifecycle_stage_config all live in the bridge schema (USAGE=service_role only); the anon/authenticated .from('bridge...') path stays unreachable, fails closed. No conservation table gains a column; no FK points from conservation into bridge. (2) RPC-only ingress, admin-gated verbatim to the existing 11-RPC pattern: record_venture_event / get_venture_lifecycle / get_grove_overview are SECURITY DEFINER, set search_path=public, call _bridge_require_admin() first, EXECUTE-granted to authenticated (never anon), fail closed. The investor/public lenses use SEPARATE money-free, name-free projection RPCs (get_venture_lifecycle_public, get_public_grove_seeds) that return only geometry (stage indices, relative sweeps, anonymised ray counts) — no fund names, no magnitude_band, no evidence_ref internals, no pipeline_stage. (3) Reads only the FROZEN snapshot, never live conservation: the pith renders from bridge.opportunities.provenance_snapshot (IUCN tier, ladder rung, endorsement count, frozen_at) already copied at door-open. Ship the explicit unit assertion the firewall critic mandated: every Cambium RPC issues ZERO live SELECTs against any public conservation table (species, programs, localities, market_intelligence). Because evidence is the gravity/centre, a venture with no frozen snapshot literally has no pith to grow around and cannot be drawn — you cannot place a body without a one-directional frozen citation first. (4) One-directional: nothing writes back into conservation; NO 'Listed on the Exchange' cite is injected into any /geocon outcome card — that remains the hard NO-BUILD from the critic verdict (it is a no-build, not a deferral). Any 'this work yielded a progressing venture' signal is a derived count produced INSIDE the bridge boundary and shown ONLY on /exchange. (5) Zero money on any surface and NONE EVEN STORED: magnitude_band is a relative enum (xs..xl); there is no price/revenue/valuation/amount column in the lifecycle table at all. Ring thickness is relative to the venture's OWN history so it can never be reverse-engineered into a figure. The only human-visible number anywhere is a COUNT (rings, backers, ventures). (6) PII stays gated: fund names on rays appear only in lens A behind _bridge_require_admin; the BEE participant role gets NO bridge USAGE and NO grant on the admin RPCs. Crosscheck the migration + the public projection RPCs with Codex before ship (firewall-critical).

## Uniqueness
It is the INVERSE topology of every circular-kanban/donut, on five axes no board can touch: (1) radius is stage and angle is TIME — a body has a real continuous 2-D position (stage x time-in-stage), not a bucket; you never move sideways, you only deposit outward. (2) Growth is MONOTONIC and irreversible — encoded by the physical impossibility of un-growing a ring, which is the append-only event log made literal; a kanban lets you drag a card backwards, the wood cannot. (3) HISTORY is the primary object, not current state — the entire stage_history is permanently legible in one figure (the founder's explicit 'how it got there' is the default view, not a drill-down), whereas a board shows only 'where it is now' and forgets the path. (4) Health is read PRE-COGNITIVELY as deformation — dendrology already trained humans to read stress years; an over-long un-closing ring = a stall, before any label, and a still grey edge = no momentum. (5) The firewall is drawn into the GEOMETRY — the evidence sits immutable, green, at the literal centre, and commerce grows outward and can never grow inward; with no frozen snapshot there is no pith and the body cannot be placed at all. And it is unmistakably Venn + on-mission: concentric rings are the program engine's X/Y/Z language applied to capital, and a stem/bulb cross-section is the right shape for an atlas of geophytes. It is a measuring instrument, not a task board.
