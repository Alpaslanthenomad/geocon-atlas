# Venn Exchange — VC strategy + P4 plan (design, for approval)

> 8-agent strategy workflow (3 ground+web research, 3 lenses, synthesis, adversarial critic). DESIGN ONLY — not built. The founder approves before any build.

## Thesis
Do not try to onboard VCs — onboard nobody; transmit to them where they already live (inbox, warm intro, a trusted curated feed). At 0 opportunities and 0 investors, "VENN is the only matchmaker" is not a weakness to fix but exactly how curated dealflow (Dealflow.eu, scout programs, DocSend/Papermark links) already works, and the firewall is the wedge: the frozen provenance_snapshot is a native, pre-validated, money-uncontaminated data room that no ordinary founder's DocSend folder can show, which is precisely what deep-science capital rewards (conviction over breadth, rigorous data, regulatory path). So the model is: VENN is the human curator who holds the directory, runs the already-built matcher silently, and hands a specific VC a thesis-matched, evidence-first, no-login one-pager in their own inbox via warm double-opt-in intro (primary) or compliance-gated cold email (secondary), with a quarterly verified-outputs digest as the inbound magnet. Everything reuses the bridge substrate already shipped (P0–P3); the two genuinely new builds are the tokenized no-login deal room with view analytics and the consent-clean OpenVC directory import, both additive, reversible, and firewall-cross-checked. No fabricated traction ever appears (imported VCs are reference data about third parties, never counted as participants or implied to have engaged; gaps render as [EKLE:], never invented numbers), and no scraping of non-public contact data occurs (OpenVC's sanctioned opt-in export is the backbone).

## VC access model
PRIMARY = DIRECT-REACH (the VC never signs up). Three deliverables leave the platform, none requiring an account. (1) Warm double-opt-in intro — the spine and highest-converting mechanic, and the one the firewall makes VENN uniquely good at. The bridge.investors.warm_intro_path field already exists but is recorded and never actuated; formalize it into a workflow where VENN sends a FORWARDABLE note to a connector, the connector forwards with one line of endorsement, and the VC opts in BEFORE the founder is exposed. Pipeline stage matched->intro does not advance until a real opted-in touch exists; a warm_intro_path of 'cold' routes to deliverable (2). (2) Tokenized no-login one-pager — the direct-reach workhorse and literal answer to 'make pitches visible/transmitted without joining': a per-investor tokenized URL renders generate_pitch_outline (the frozen-snapshot one-pager) as a page; the VC clicks, reads in-browser, never signs up; VENN sees open/dwell/forward analytics so intro->nda becomes data-driven (a VC who rereads the evidence section twice is hot). The one-pager is the OPENING instrument (kill-shot = a 3-4 bullet evidence box: IUCN tier + verification ladder + endorsement count + DOI); the full deck is the closing instrument, held for after a bite. (3) The quarterly digest (see below). The matchmaker is the founder + an AI: the machine does matching, drafting, link-minting, open-tracking; the founder does only the irreducibly human acts — deciding which outcome is real enough to open a door, approving/sending the actual intro, taking the call when a VC bites. SECONDARY = INBOUND (bring the willing ones in, never a hard signup). (a) The dealflow digest: a quarterly 'N new verified value-outputs in [vertical]' email to a hand-built biodiversity/biotech/impact list, each item a teaser of a frozen outcome linking to its no-login page — integrity-safe because only verified outcomes are ever published, so it can never become fabricated traction. (b) An invite-only virtual showcase: a handful of verified opportunities pitched to a curated investor list, zero onboarding, creating the warm context that makes the follow-up one-pager actually get opened. (c) If a VC genuinely wants in: a thin curated participant view (P4 public board, designed not built) shows the deal grid filtered by their thesis with a Venn-mediated 'Request warm intro' CTA — never direct contact. Launch curated-invite first; flip to logged-in-public only once the pipeline is real.

## Portal design
The portal is a PRODUCTION LINE that turns a venn_verified outcome into a transmittable evidence-first artifact — not a destination VCs log into. Three layers, all in the bridge schema, additive/reversible. LAYER A — LISTING CONTENT MODEL (grown incrementally; ship thin first). Today an opportunity holds {title, commercial_application_md, need_kind, stage, provenance_snapshot} — ~5% of a VC-credible artifact. Add six blocks, each split into a frozen-evidence half (auto-pulled, immutable) and an admin-narrative half (typed, [EKLE:]-able): Block 1 THE ASK (structured ask_kind/ask_amount_cents/ask_currency/ask_use_of_funds_md/ask_timeline_months/ip_status — replaces vague need_kind; ask_amount is appetite on the commerce side only, never deal value, never cited back); Block 2 THE TEAM (the single biggest current absence — but NOT a live join: freeze provenance_snapshot.team[]={display_name,role,orcid,affiliation,public_profile_path} from the outcome's commercialization_credits at door-open, only already-public attribution, no email ever frozen in); Block 3 COMMERCIAL THESIS (market_thesis_md + buyer_segments[]; TAM only if sourced or rendered [EKLE: market size]; this is the firewall-relocated home for species.market_* which moves to the bridge side); Block 4 EVIDENCE/PROVENANCE (already strong — surface harder: IUCN badge, verification-ladder badge reusing OutcomesRoute TIERS, endorsement count, frozen_at + freshness flag, DOI links; say explicitly the evidence is frozen/immutable so a VC's diligence team can trust it was not money-edited); Block 5 THE ONE-PAGER promoted from ephemeral to a governed versioned artifact in new table bridge.opportunity_pitch (version, body_md, status draft|ready, has_unfilled_placeholders, generated_from_frozen_at) — CANNOT be marked ready while any [EKLE:]/[ADD:] remains (server-side scan = the integrity gate); Block 6 STAGE-WITH-EVIDENCE (freeze provenance_snapshot.lineage.stage_history[] from the program's evidenced tics so 'prototype' is provable, not admin-typed). LAYER B — PROJECT-OWNER SIDE. Owners cannot self-post (that would let unverified commerce onto the board); instead a thin proposal queue feeds the one firewall door. Owner declares the outcome in the existing CommercializedOutcomes panel (conservation-side, money-free), it climbs the verification ladder, and on reaching venn_verified the owner can submit a listing intent (commercial idea + uploaded pitch PDF in a private service-role bucket exchange-decks) into new table bridge.listing_requests — which does NOT create a listing; it lands in the admin desk, VENN reviews, and if approved calls the existing open_bridge_opportunity (the freeze still happens through the one true seam). LAYER C — THE VC-FACING DEAL ROOM (centerpiece). New public route app/exchange/deal/[token]/page.js, outside GeoconShell, outside auth, robots:noindex, rendered server-side from the token only. New table bridge.deal_links (token PK, opportunity_id, investor_id nullable, pitch_version pinned to status=ready, expires_at default 30d, revoked) — one token per (opportunity,investor) so analytics attribute per-VC and a leaked link revokes without killing others; minting REFUSES if no ready (placeholder-free) pitch exists. Served by a single new SECURITY DEFINER get_deal_room(p_token) — the ONE deliberately anon-granted bridge read: token-gated, returns a fixed allow-list of fields (the listing blocks + the pinned ready pitch), no contact_email/PII, no pipeline, no fit_scores, no other investors/listings, no live conservation read (frozen-only). It SHOWS the ask, frozen team, thesis, the evidence wall, stage timeline, the one-pager inline + 'Download PDF' of that version, and a single 'Request a conversation with VENN' CTA (mediated, never direct founder contact). View tracking via bridge.deal_link_events + anon-callable append-only log_deal_link_event(token,event) feeds the curator's matched->intro->meeting decisions with observed behavior (not fabricated traction). TRANSMISSION: per-VC mint + relevance-led email drafted by generate_outreach_email(opp,investor) from the fit_score breakdown (legal_basis-gated, DE/FR/ES flagged warm-intro-only), plus the quarterly digest via get_exchange_digest_items(since,vertical_ids) returning only ready+fresh listings with zero money figures, pg_cron-drafted but always admin-approved before send.

## Data-pull design
A VC directory is a THIRD LIFECYCLE LAYER on the already-built bridge.investors table — the matcher already returns directory candidates correctly (no lifecycle filter, scores by tag overlap, gates on vertical_focus), so the directory plugs into matching for FREE; the work is in provenance columns, lifecycle status, an outreach log, and surface-gating, NOT new matching logic. SCHEMA (one reversible DDL): add to bridge.investors — lifecycle text default 'engaged' CHECK(directory|engaged) (the directory-vs-participant distinction; hand-added rows stay 'engaged', only the importer writes 'directory'); source text (openvc_export|public_site|manual); source_url text (the exact page a thesis/stage/ticket was read from, so every claim is re-verifiable, never invented); last_verified_at timestamptz (dated provenance — surface staleness, re-verify before outreach); legal_basis text CHECK(opt_in|legitimate_interest|warm_intro_only); opted_out_at timestamptz (hard permanent suppression = the one-click GDPR opt-out); external_ref text + a partial unique index on (source, external_ref) so re-import upserts idempotently and never duplicates. THREE STATES governed by lifecycle x opted_out_at: directory (imported reference data, visible ONLY in the admin desk as a match candidate, never on a public board, never counted in get_exchange_public_stats, never implied to have engaged); engaged (a real opted-in touch exists); suppressed (opted_out_at set — filtered everywhere forever). The promotion event is the integrity hinge: a small RPC engage_investor(id) flips directory->engaged ONLY when a real outbound touch is logged, never on match — keeping 'we matched you' (a computed suggestion) structurally separate from 'they're a participant' (an earned fact). MATCHER PATCH (surgical, edits one existing function): add `AND i.opted_out_at IS NULL` (the only mandatory change); return i.lifecycle/source/last_verified_at/legal_basis/warm_intro_path so candidate cards show provenance; add a SOFT ticket-band scoring term (penalty not exclusion: ask within band +15, far outside -10, unknown 0) so VENN doesn't bug a $500M fund with a $5M ask while a near-miss still surfaces ranked lower; fit_score stays explicitly a computed suggestion in the UI. OUTREACH LOG (the actuation layer that's currently dark): new table bridge.investor_outreach (investor_id, opportunity_id nullable, channel warm_intro|tokenized_link|digest|email, legal_basis snapshot at send, status queued->sent->opened->replied->opted_out/bounced, token unique, sent_at, responded_at). Any row hitting status=opted_out stamps investors.opted_out_at=now() so the VC vanishes from matching/outreach permanently. RPCs (all SECURITY DEFINER, search_path=public, _bridge_require_admin-gated except the token-scoped event logger): log_outreach (inserts row, mints token, calls engage_investor), record_outreach_event(token,event), suppress_investor(id), ingest_investor_batch(p_rows jsonb) (validates + idempotent upsert by external_ref). INGEST (Python, already installed): OpenVC CSV -> normalize -> filter to verticals (biotech/climate/nature-tech/beauty-ingredients) -> ingest_investor_batch, stamped source=openvc_export, legal_basis=opt_in, lifecycle=directory. Backbone = OpenVC because it is free, exportable BY DESIGN into your own CRM, and its listed investors have OPTED IN to cold inbound — sidestepping most of the GDPR problem. Hand-curate ~15-20 named funds from their public sites (Superorganism, Planet A, Pale Blue Dot, Sofinnova Industrial Biotech, Forbion BioEconomy, the cosmetics/ingredient set) as source=public_site, legal_basis=legitimate_interest (or warm_intro_only if DE/FR/ES). Crunchbase/Dealroom/PitchBook = read-only enrichment to verify a row you already hold, NEVER stored as source of record (their licenses forbid copying). Geography->legal_basis auto-maps on ingest so DE/FR/ES rows are forced to warm_intro_only and can never be cold-emailed. No scraping, no list-buy, no list-rental.

## Firewall & compliance
FIREWALL (preserve verbatim, widen by zero new money paths). 1) Every listing/deal-room/matcher/outreach RPC reads ONLY bridge rows or the frozen provenance_snapshot — NO live SELECT against conservation tables (species, programs, localities, market_intelligence). team[] and stage_history[] are frozen COPIES of already-public attribution taken at the one door (open_bridge_opportunity), refreshed only by explicit admin refresh_opportunity_snapshot — adding zero new conservation->commerce read path. Carry the unit assertion the firewall critic requires: 'none of the new RPCs issue a live SELECT against public conservation tables.' 2) get_deal_room and log_deal_link_event are the ONLY anon-granted bridge functions — both read-only-or-append, token-gated, PII-free, frozen-only; everything else stays behind _bridge_require_admin, granted to authenticated only, failing closed. Never grant bridge USAGE to anon/authenticated. 3) contact_email and all participant PII stay admin-gated, never on a deal room, digest, or public route. 4) ask_amount/ticket_* are appetite on the commerce side only — no deal value, price, or revenue ever appears on a listing, and nothing money-shaped is cited back into a /geocon surface (the critic's NO-BUILD on 'Listed on the Exchange' cite-back stands hard, not deferred). 5) bridge.investors and bridge.investor_outreach are pure commerce-side, one-directional; no conservation table ever gains an investor or money column. 6) PREREQUISITE from the critic verdict: the P0 'zero money in conservation' invariant is FALSE as written (legacy anon-readable market_intelligence/species.market_*/species_stories.market_narrative/commercial.market_type still hold money columns). The de-commercialization of SpeciesDetailPanel + revokes are already done (THE-EXCHANGE cleanup status 2026-06-09); the remaining column move (species.market_* + species_stories.market_narrative -> bridge side) must complete before Block 3 surfaces market data, and the firewall regression test must enumerate exactly which public columns are allowed to be money/market and fail on any NEW one — never assert a blanket zero. MANDATORY /crosscheck (Codex, read-only) before shipping: get_deal_room (the only anon bridge read), the per-country email gate, and the market-column relocation migration — these are the surfaces that render conservation-cited evidence to a money audience, allowed ONLY because they pull from the frozen snapshot, carry no money column back, and the cite is one-directional. COMPLIANCE (VENN = Estonian/EU controller). Two-tier rule: firm-level facts (fund/thesis/stage/ticket/public submissions email) = low risk; personal contact_email needs a basis carried per-row in legal_basis. Write a one-page LIA once, dated before any outreach, covering legitimate_interest rows. Per-country gate enforced in code: log_outreach REFUSES channel=email/digest for warm_intro_only rows so DE/FR/ES are structurally warm-intro-only. Every outbound email (enforced by template, not left to the human) carries: a one-click opt-out honored before the next send, VENN's registered Estonian sender identity + address, a one-line relevance statement, a privacy-policy link naming legitimate interest, and the recorded source. The investor_outreach row IS the record of where the contact came from. INTEGRITY: importing 16,000 OpenVC rows inflates ZERO public numbers (lifecycle=directory is hard-excluded from get_exchange_public_stats and every public board); every thesis/stage/ticket is source+source_url+last_verified_at stamped (null beats guess); fit_score is labelled a computed suggestion; a VC becomes visible/engaged ONLY after a real logged opted-in touch.
