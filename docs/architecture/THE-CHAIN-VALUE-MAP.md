# From an endangered geophyte to every value it can yield

> The architecture, explained. Synthesised 2026-06-07 from the 279-node expansion
> brainstorm + grown by inference into the full value map. This is what the deep
> thinking actually produced — NOT six words. The six labels were a premature
> placeholder; the real result is the branching graph below. Vocabulary for the
> spine is deferred. Status: **design / explanation**.

---

## 0. The correction

A chain of six words (taxonomy → … → metabolites) was the wrong picture. The
brainstorm produced a **branching knowledge-and-value graph**: ~**279 nodes**
today across **7 great branches**, open-ended (any new topic attaches as data).
Every node is *simultaneously* a **knowledge question** (academic value) and a
**value pathway** (end-product value), with **conservation** woven through as the
guardrail. The endangered plant enters as a name in a flora; the architecture maps
every thread of knowledge and every value that can flow from it — without
destroying the wild plant.

Two missions, one graph:
- **CONSERVE** — protect the wild species (IUCN, threats, in-situ/ex-situ, policy).
- **CREATE VALUE** — academic value (knowledge commons) **and** end-product value
  (bioeconomy), kept firewalled from the conservation data by schema.

The hinge that makes them one act, not opposites: **propagation + cultivation +
biosynthesis** let you produce the value *without* wild harvest — so value funds
conservation instead of looting it.

## 1. The 7 great branches (the 279, organised)

Each branch is a sub-graph. For each: what it is, the **academic value** it yields,
the **product value** it enables, and where GEOCON stands today.

### 1 · IDENTITY & SYSTEMATICS (~39 nodes) — *who it is*
Nomenclature & typification · voucher/specimen evidence · DNA barcoding &
phylogenetics · morphology, anatomy & cytology (the storage-organ that defines a
geophyte; chromosome counts, ploidy).
- **Academic value:** accepted names, type designations, barcodes, phylogenies,
  genome/plastome accessions — citable, the root every other branch keys off.
- **Product value:** authentication & anti-adulteration (proving a traded "saffron"
  or "salep" is the real species), provenance for legal trade.
- **Today:** names ✓ (un-decomposed); cytology/DNA mostly absent but harvestable.
- *Geophyte reason it matters most here:* Crocus, Allium, Fritillaria, Galanthus,
  Tulipa are cryptic-species, ploidy-variable — identity is unstable exactly for
  the CR endemics, and a wrong name silently mis-attributes every downstream value.

### 2 · ECOLOGY & DISTRIBUTION (~62 nodes) — *where & how it lives*
Biogeography & range · habitat & soil · bioclimatic niche & climate-change
vulnerability · phenology (the geophyte dormancy clock) · pollination & reproductive
ecology · seed & germination ecology · population demography · symbioses
(mycorrhiza, microbiome) · in-situ threats.
- **Academic value:** SDMs, phenology, pollination biology, demography.
- **Conservation value:** the evidence base for protection (AOO/EOO, trend).
- **Product value:** the niche it needs *is the recipe to cultivate it* — soil,
  climate, dormancy cues feed straight into the cultivation branch.
- **Today:** native countries/regions ✓ partial; the rest largely empty.

### 3 · CONSERVATION & POLICY (~38 nodes) — *protect it*
Red List assessment & criteria · threats (habitat loss, **over-collection/poaching**,
climate) · in-situ (protected-area overlap, monitoring) · **ex-situ & germplasm**
(seed banks, living collections, cryo) · recovery (reintroduction) · legal & access
governance (legislation, CITES, **Nagoya/ABS**, FairWild).
- **Academic value:** IUCN assessments, conservation-genetics studies.
- **Mission value:** this is the conservation mission's home — its own integrity rail.
- **Product link:** germplasm (seed banks / accessions) is the one node where
  conservation insurance and production feedstock are the *same* rows.
- **Today:** IUCN status ✓ for ~423 of 47k (the known gap); CITES ✓ partial.

### 4 · PROPAGATION & CULTIVATION (~36 nodes) — *make more of it, sustainably*
Seed / vegetative (offsets, scaling, twin-scaling) / **in-vitro micropropagation** ·
dormancy breaking & storage · husbandry (substrate, water, nutrition, light) ·
pest/disease & virus indexing · domestication · breeding & cultivar development ·
agronomy & yield · post-harvest.
- **Academic value:** propagation & micropropagation protocols (highly publishable,
  thesis-sized, *student-fillable*), breeding studies.
- **Product value:** **the supply chain that does NOT loot the wild** — the bridge
  between conservation and value. If you can micropropagate a CR Galanthus, you can
  sell galantamine without digging up the mountain.
- **Today:** essentially empty — the founder's "empty middle", and the highest-
  leverage place to pull students in.

### 5 · CHEMISTRY & BIOACTIVITY (~28 nodes) — *what's in it & what it does*
Tissue sourcing (organ-specific — a bulb ≠ its leaves) · extraction · isolation &
fractionation · structure elucidation (NMR/MS) · phytochemical profiling
(HPLC/GC-MS) · untargeted metabolomics · **bioactivity → target → mechanism** ·
toxicology/safety · standardization & QC · chemotype variability · biosynthesis.
- **Academic value:** compound isolation, structures, bioassays, metabolomes.
- **Product value:** **the molecules themselves** — the active that becomes a drug,
  a cosmetic ingredient, a nutraceutical.
- **Today:** metabolites ✓ for 319 species; extraction/bioactivity mostly unstructured.

### 6 · OMICS & GENETICS (~43 nodes) — *the molecular machinery*
Genome / transcriptome / proteome · **gene → enzyme → pathway** (biosynthesis
genetics) · molecular markers · population & conservation genetics · gene-bank
genomics · functional genomics (CRISPR, heterologous expression) · genotype →
chemotype.
- **Academic value:** genomes, elucidated biosynthetic pathways — the deepest,
  most defensible science assets.
- **Product value:** **alternative supply** — express the pathway in yeast/cell
  culture and make the compound with *no plant at all*; marker-assisted breeding.
- **Today:** near-absent; high-value, largely harvestable from GenBank/Kew.

### 7 · TRANSLATION & TRADITIONAL KNOWLEDGE (~33 nodes) — *turn it into value*
Ethnobotany & traditional/Indigenous use (**IPLC-governed, ABS-gated**) ·
bioactivity → pre-application evidence (pharmacology, toxicology, nutrition,
sensory) · **the application fan** (pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, cosmeceutical,
ornamental, food, agricultural, industrial, fragrance) · products, cultivars & IP
(PBR, patents, GI) · supply chain & sustainable sourcing · benefit-sharing.
- **Academic value:** ethnobotany, pharmacology, application studies.
- **Product value:** **the end-product destinations themselves** — the value
  mission's home, firewalled from the conservation spine.
- **Today:** market-area fields ✓ sparse; mostly to-build, ABS-gated.

## 2. The two value tracks (both, as the founder insists)

**ACADEMIC value — the knowledge commons.** Every filled node is a citable
contribution: peer-reviewed papers, theses (BSc → MSc → PhD → postdoc), DOIs &
datasets, IUCN assessments, DNA barcodes & genomes, propagation protocols,
reference standards, elucidated pathways. This is what excites students,
academics and independents, and it is what builds GEOCON's scientific authority.
*Mechanism: a node moves from empty → evidenced by an attributed, citable Move.*

**END-PRODUCT value — the bioeconomy.** A geophyte that is identified, cultivable,
characterised and de-risked becomes a real product. The destinations:

| Destination | Real geophyte example |
|---|---|
| **Pharmaceutical** | **Galantamine** for Alzheimer's, from *Galanthus* / *Leucojum* (Amaryllidaceae). **Colchicine** for gout, from *Colchicum*. Lycorine/haemanthamine — anticancer leads. |
| **Nutraceutical / functional food** | **Saffron** (*Crocus sativus*) — crocin/safranal, antidepressant & antioxidant evidence. |
| **Cosmeceutical** | Bulb-extract anti-aging actives (*Galanthus*, *Narcissus*, *Lilium*). |
| **Ornamental / horticulture** | The bulb trade — tulips, *Fritillaria*, *Galanthus*, *Cyclamen* (a multi-billion-euro industry). |
| **Food** | Salep (orchid tubers) — *and a cautionary tale*: wild harvest for value drove the species toward collapse. The chain's answer is cultivation. |
| **Agricultural / biostimulant, fragrance, industrial** | Extracts, essential compounds, biomaterials. |

## 3. The weave — why conservation and value are ONE act (grown by inference)

The flagship story, made concrete: wild *Galanthus* is CITES-listed and was
over-collected because its value (galantamine) was harvested from the mountain.
The naive view: value vs conservation, enemies. **The chain's answer:** walk the
species down the branches — identity → ecology → **propagation → cultivation** →
chemistry → application → and, via **omics, biosynthesis in a bioreactor** — and
the value is produced *without touching the wild population*. Value then **funds**
conservation (royalties → ABS benefit-sharing → seed banks → reintroduction)
instead of destroying it. That inversion — *value as the engine of conservation* —
is VENN's mission expressed as architecture, and it lives in branches 4 (cultivate)
and 6 (biosynthesise), firewalled from the IUCN data by schema.

## 4. The layers (how it stacks, endangered plant → value)

A geophyte ascends through layers; each layer is buildable on the one below and
valuable on its own:

```
 L-id   IDENTITY        know what it is              (academic: names, barcodes)
 L-eco  LIFE            know where/how it lives       (conservation evidence)
 L-safe CONSERVATION    protect + bank it             (the guardrail rail)
 L-prop SUPPLY          propagate + cultivate it      (value WITHOUT wild harm)
 L-chem SUBSTANCE       characterise its chemistry    (the molecules)
 L-val  VALUE           bioactivity → application      (academic + end-product)
 ──────  OMICS threads through every layer; CONSERVATION runs as a parallel rail;
         the APPLICATION fan funnels to the firewalled Ventures layer ─────────
```

Each layer is a cluster of the 279 nodes. A student fills one node; a thesis a few;
a program orchestrates a layer; a grant funds a gap; industry reads the maturity.
Every advance is academic value *and* a step toward end-product value.

## 5. Status & the open question

This is the architecture — the multi-branched map from an endangered plant to every
value it can yield, conservation and value woven, academic and product value both.
What is **deferred** is the *vocabulary*: the names for the spine and branches (the
"6 words" problem) — equivalent-altitude, non-limiting, genuinely chaining words.
Once those are settled we seed the link-type registry with the real branch names
and rebuild the species surface to show this map honestly.

The 279 are not a build list; they are the **possibility space**. We build the
spine + a handful of high-leverage nodes first, and the graph grows by INSERT —
forever — as the community fills it.
